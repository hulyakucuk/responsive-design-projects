Cahit Arf

Cahit arf's Life:

  • 1910:Cahit Arf was born on 11 October 1910[1] in Selanik (Thessaloniki), which was then a part of the Ottoman Empire.
  • 1912:His family migrated to Istanbul with the outbreak of the Balkan War.
  • 1913:he joined the Mathematics Department of Istanbul University.
  • 1962:He returned to Istanbul University and worked there until his involvement with the foundation work of Scientific and Technological Research Council (TÜBİTAK) upon President Cemal Gürsel's appointment in 1962.
  • 1980:Upon his final return to Turkey, he joined the Mathematics Department of the Middle East Technical University and continued his studies there until his retirement in 1980
  • 1948:Arf received numerous awards for his contributions to mathematics, among them are: İnönü Award in 1948
  • 1974: Scientific and Technological Research Council of Turkey (TÜBİTAK) Science Award in 1974.
  • 1997:Arf died on December 26, 1997 in Bebek, Istanbul, at the age of 87.
